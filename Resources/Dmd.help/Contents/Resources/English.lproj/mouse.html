About the Mosue

You may notice that the cursor on the emulated DMD 5620 sometimes does not match up perfectly with the Macintosh pointer. This is a known problem.

Unfortunately, there is no way to fix this. The terminal's ROM program always interprets mouse position in a relative way, not as fixed points, so the cursors can come out of alignment.

To work around this problem, “chase” the DMD 5620 cursor to the top of the screen with the Macintosh pointer until the two are aligned again. Then, they should stay in alignment.
