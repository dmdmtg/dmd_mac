The DMD 5620 terminal on its own is not very interesting. To get the most out of it, you need a host system that understands the DMD’s custom windowing protocol.

At the moment, probably the best way to use the DMD 5620 emulator is in combination with an AT&T 3B2/400 emulator running the AT&T DMD Utilities.
